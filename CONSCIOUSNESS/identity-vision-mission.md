## Identity

A university-grade reinforcement learning curriculum that teaches agents, policies, value functions, and policy-gradient methods through mathematical derivation and from-scratch implementation.

## Vision

A complete RL curriculum from Markov decision processes through deep RL (DQN, PPO, SAC) — same rigour and format as the supervised and unsupervised series.

## Mission

Derives the Bellman equations, implements agents from scratch, and applies production frameworks — one algorithm per lesson, theory and practical notebooks per lesson.
