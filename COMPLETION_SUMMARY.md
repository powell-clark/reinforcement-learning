## 💡 Key Insights Learned

### Most Important Algorithms (2025)
1. **PPO** - General purpose, 80% of use cases
2. **SAC** - Best for robotics/continuous control
3. **RLHF** - Critical for LLM alignment ⭐⭐⭐

### Critical Concepts
1. **Bellman Equations** - Foundation of all RL
2. **Policy Gradient Theorem** - Basis of modern methods
3. **Experience Replay** - Makes DQN stable
4. **Trust Regions** - PPO's key insight
5. **Entropy Regularization** - SAC's advantage
6. **Preference Learning** - RLHF's breakthrough

### Production Lessons
1. Start simple (random → baseline → full)
2. Hyperparameters matter more than architecture
3. Debugging is systematic, not trial-and-error
4. Monitor everything (not just reward)
5. Safety first (constraints, fallbacks, A/B tests)
